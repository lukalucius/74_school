74 school

The school was founded in 1938. Over the years, the school has changed its name several times. There was the 9th high school for boys, the N2 high school of A/C Railway, the N74 high school of Tbilisi, and since 2005 the N74 public school of the city of Tbilisi. Despite the age of the building, the school has a cozy and comfortable environment. It has outdoor and indoor sports ground, educational laboratories, library, modern electronic information and educational facilities. The school works in two shifts. 638 students study in the current academic year, 22 financial classes were opened. The school has 42 teachers, 16 of them are certified. You will meet very friendly children and teachers who are masters of their craft. The headmaster of the school is Marine Jarmelishvili.
